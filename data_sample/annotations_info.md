# 📊 Training Dataset Information

This model was trained using a custom blend of the **TACO (Trash Annotations in Context)** and **TrashNet** datasets.

### Key Stats:
- **Total Images:** 1,500+
- **Environment:** Real-world cluttered backgrounds (Campus, Streets, Parks)
- **Classes:** Biodegradable, Recyclable, Hazardous, Landfill
- **Augmentation:** Random flip, brightness adjustment, and scaling to increase robustness.
